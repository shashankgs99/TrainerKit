Virtual Digital IC Trainer Kit

2-input quad AND gate IC

2-input quad OR gate IC

2-input quad NAND gate IC

2-input quad NOR gate IC

2-input quad XOR gate IC

Instructions

  • Set the inputs.
  • Connect the VCC and ground.
  • Turn on the power button to display output.
  • Restart the power after making any changes.

Color Codes

  • VCC is red toggle button. Ground is black toggle button.
  • Inputs are blue toggle button.
  • Red light indicates that output is bit '0'.
  • Green light indicates that output is bit '1'
